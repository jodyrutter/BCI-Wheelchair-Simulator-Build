1. Before opening, please open the dll folder and make a copy of the dlls on your PC (this will depend on whether your PC is 32 or 64 bit). 
2. Next, right click either 'My Computer' or 'This PC.'
3. Click 'properties.'
4. Click 'Advanced system settings.'
5. Click 'Environmental Variables.'
6. Add a new system variable and name it 'EMOTIV.'
7. Then, link that variable to where your dlls were coppied to.
8. Click 'ok.'
9. Open the program.
